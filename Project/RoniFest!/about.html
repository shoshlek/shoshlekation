About Us

Have you ever got annoyed by the fact that all major music festivals are being held in Europe or USA?
Good! We're here to end your frustration!
Join us for the first music festival in Israel that includes the greatest bands in the world. We'll provide camping areas, food stands, facilities, merchandise, drinks and tons of rock!

Roni Granit is a true rock fan. She started out as a punk and discovered the gods of rock, metal, blues and so much more. Roni's dream was to be in a rock concert. One day, she flew to the States and met Green Day at a small venue. They asked her if there where any rock festivals in Israel and she sadly relpy - no. Roni never gave up. She used up all her connections in the music world until she was finally able to bring this event to life!
